My Work

I am working with LLVM backend development and writing the tutorial step by step following the LLVM front end style. Since LLVM backend lacks this material, the LLVM backend programmers cannot enjoy the happy learning experience like front end. I am proud of my work is accepted by LLVM documentation, appears at http://llvm.org/docs/tutorial/#external-tutorials. Hoping it can help LLVM backend programmer learning backend as easy/happy as front end.
